## 📁 Projects

### 1. Volcanic Sand Soap Funnel
**File:** `volcanic-sand-funnel.html`

A complete marketing funnel for Aruba Aloe's Volcanic Sand Soap Bar implementing 10 dark marketing patterns:

- ⏰ Urgency (countdown timers)
- 📦 Scarcity (stock levels)
- 👥 Social Proof (live notifications)
- 👑 Authority (credentials)
- 💰 Price Anchoring
- 😫 Pain Point Agitation
- 🛡️ Risk Reversal
- 🛑 Exit-Intent Capture
- 📊 Comparison Tables
- 🎁 Reciprocity

**Demo:** Open `volcanic-sand-funnel.html` in browser
